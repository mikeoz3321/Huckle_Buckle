1) Computer generates a number
2) User enters first guess
3) Computer checks whether the guess is right
	a) Correct guess - "You Win!". Games Ends
	b) Incorrect guess - computer tells you whether you are hot, cold, warm, depending on how far your guess is form the computer's number
4) User enters second guess
	a) Correct guess - "You win!". Game Ends
	b) Incorrect guess - computer tells youw wheter you are going in the right direction. "Getting Warmer, getting colder, getting hot"
5) Repeat #4 until Game ends
